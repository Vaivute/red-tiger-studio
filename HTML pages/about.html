Hi, I'm the tiger behind the red.

Red Tiger Studio started in a small Seattle apartment with a kitchen table, a stack of paper and a question. What if art felt less like a gallery visit and more like a letter from a friend?

The subscription is the answer. Every month, I make a new piece, sign it, number it and post it to members across the US. No algorithm picks it. No factory prints it. It's just me, a studio and a pile of rigid mailers.

My work sits somewhere between still life and folklore. Bold colour. Loose line. A bit of bite. If you've seen my Instagram, you know the feel. If you haven't, the next print in your mailbox is the best introduction.

I believe original art should be something you live with, not something you save for. That's why the subscription is eight dollars, and why I cap founding membership at a hundred people. Real relationships, not reach.

If you collect with me, you're buying more than a print. You're keeping an independent studio running from the Pacific Northwest, one envelope at a time. Thank you for being here.
