Questions

The fine print.

When does my print ship?

Prints ship on the first working day of each month. You'll get a tracking email when yours goes out. Delivery takes three to five working days across the US.

Can I cancel any time?

Yes. Manage your subscription from your account page. Cancel, pause or skip a month whenever you like. No questions, no hoops.

What if I miss a month?

Past drops go into the catch-up store after 60 days. You'll always have a way to complete the collection, though some months will sell out.

Can I gift a subscription?

Yes. Pick the annual plan and enter the recipient's address at checkout. We'll send a welcome card letting them know it's from you.

What size are the prints?

Prints are 5×7 inches on archival cotton rag paper. They fit standard frames and mailers arrive rigid, so nothing bends.

Do you ship internationally?

US only for now. International shipping arrives in early 2026. Sign up to the newsletter to hear first.
